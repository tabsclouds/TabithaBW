Statement

Tabitha Beresford-Webb tabithabw5@gmail.com

My artistic practice explores how I perceive my physical interaction with digital/virtual images by creating videos that explore my visual experiences with digital and non-fixed images (e.g. reflections). My animations explore the cartoon-like experience of interacting with digital images through the actions of Charac and Other (my two cartoon characters), who treat the digital image as a cartoon object. Discussions with scientists regarding this topic have revealed that the scientific explanations of human-digital interaction do not fully encompass the immediate, instinctive human experience. My artistic methods promote an artistic, cartoon approach to investigating how I perceive digital images visually and physically.
